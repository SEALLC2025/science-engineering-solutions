About Us

With more than a decade of academic research in microplastics and emerging chemicals of concern, we witnessed firsthand the growing challenges faced by industry and by water and wastewater utilities in addressing microplastic pollution and its potential impacts on water and food safety. As regulatory interest intensified and the need for reliable analytical methods increased, it became clear that a gap existed between cutting-edge scientific knowledge and the practical tools available to environmental professionals and industries.
